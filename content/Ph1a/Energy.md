# Conservation


A powerful invariance.

$$
E = K + U
$$

$K$ is kinetic energy, $U$ is potential energy.

$$
\begin{gather}
K = \frac{1}{2}m\vec{v}^{2} \\
v = v_{x} + v_{y} + v_{z}
\end{gather}
$$

# Force

$$
F = - \frac{dU}{dx}
$$

## Examples

1. Spring force

$$
F_{\text{Hooke}} = -kx, \, U = \frac{1}{2}kx^{2}
$$

2. Earth's Gravity

$$
F_{g} = -mg, \, U = mgy
$$

3. Gravity

$$
F_{g} = - \frac{GMm}{r^{2}}, \, U = - \frac{GMm}{r}
$$
